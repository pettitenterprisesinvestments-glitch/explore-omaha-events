# Papillion Days 2026: The Best FREE Family Festival Near Omaha This Father's Day Weekend

If you're looking for **free things to do in Omaha with kids this weekend**, look no further — **Papillion Days 2026** is here, and it's one of the metro area's most beloved family traditions.

Running **June 17–21, 2026** at City Park in Papillion, Nebraska, this annual celebration has been a community cornerstone since **1947**. That's nearly 80 years of parades, fireworks, and pure family fun. This year marks the **79th Annual Papillion Days**, and it's bigger than ever.

---

## What's Happening at Papillion Days 2026?

### 🎆 Fireworks Show
One of the best **free fireworks displays near Omaha** — the Papillion Days fireworks light up the sky Friday night. Best viewing spot: west of 84th Street near City Park. Bring blankets, arrive early, and find your spot before it fills up!

### 🎉 Parade
The iconic **Papillion Days Parade** rolls through Downtown Papillion on Saturday morning. Floats, bands, community groups, and of course — candy for the kids! A classic small-town parade experience right in the Omaha metro.

### 🎡 Carnival & Market
Carnival rides, games, and a local artisan market fill the park all weekend. Wristbands available for unlimited ride access — perfect for families with young kids who want to ride all day.

### 🎵 Family Fun Night
Wednesday, June 17 kicks things off with Family Fun Night from **4–8 PM** at Downtown Papillion City Park. Live entertainment, food vendors, and community fun — a great low-key start to the week's celebrations.
