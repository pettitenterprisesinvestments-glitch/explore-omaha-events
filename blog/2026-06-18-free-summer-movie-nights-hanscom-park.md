## Pro Tips for the Perfect Omaha Summer Movie Night

**Arrive early.** Good spots fill up by 7:15 PM. Aim for 6:45 PM to claim a comfortable spot with a good view.

**Bring layers.** June evenings in Omaha cool down once the sun sets. A light jacket or hoodie is smart thinking, especially for little ones.

**Pack smart snacks.** While concessions are available (and they're reasonably priced!), you can also bring your own. Pro tip: bring wet wipes for sticky fingers post-popcorn.

**Make it a picnic.** Bring a small cooler with juice boxes and sandwiches. Turn it into a full pre-movie picnic experience—kids love the adventure of it.

**Bathroom reconnaissance.** Know where the bathrooms are before the movie starts. This matters with young kids.

**Silence your phone.** Out of respect for fellow movie-goers, keep phones on silent and avoid bright screens during the film.
