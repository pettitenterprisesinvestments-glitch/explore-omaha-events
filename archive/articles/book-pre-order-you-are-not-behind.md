# You Are Not Behind — The Book Is Coming

There is a specific kind of tired that nobody talks about.

It is not the tired that comes from working too hard. It is the tired that comes from wondering — quietly, in the back of your mind, while you are folding laundry or sitting in the school pickup line — whether what you are building is actually going to work.

You started something. Maybe a business. Maybe a side income. Maybe just a vision of a life that looks different from the one you are living right now. And most days you keep going. But some days, you wonder.

*This book is for those days.*

---

## What This Book Is About

"You Are Not Behind" is a collection of honest, practical, and deeply encouraging essays for parents who are building something — a business, a brand, a better financial future — while also raising kids, managing a household, and trying not to lose themselves in the process.

It is not a hustle book. It is not a get-rich-quick playbook. It is a book about showing up — imperfectly, consistently, and with purpose — even when you are scared.

Topics include:

1. Why money fear is not a character flaw — and what to do about it 2. The small financial wins that actually matter when you are starting from behind 3. How to build a business while being present for your family 4. What your kids are really learning when they watch you try 5. Legal and financial protection every family builder needs to know 6. The mindset shift that separates people who quit from people who make it

---

## Who It Is For

This book is for you if:

You are a parent trying to build a side income or small business and you are not sure it is going to work.

You wake up at 2 AM thinking about money.

You want to model something real for your kids — not just tell them to dream big, but actually show them what it looks like to try.

You have started something and you are still in the middle of it, and you need someone to tell you that the middle is not the end.
