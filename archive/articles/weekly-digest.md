# Best Family Events in Omaha This Summer (June–October 2026)

**Looking for things to do in Omaha with kids this summer?** You've come to the right place. We've rounded up the best family-friendly, faith-positive, and completely kid-approved events happening across the Omaha metro area from now through fall. Whether you're a local family or visiting Nebraska, this is your go-to guide.

---

## 🎭 Theater & Shows the Whole Family Will Love

**Disney's Frozen at The Rose Theater** (through June 28) Anna, Elsa, Olaf, and Sven take the stage in Omaha's beloved Rose Theater. This is a *must* for families with young kids — stunning costumes, gorgeous music, and a story about love and family that never gets old. Tickets run $25–$30. 📍 The Rose Theater, 2001 Farnam St, Omaha

**Broadway's Wicked at the Orpheum** (through July 5) Omaha's most popular Broadway run is still going! If your family hasn't seen Wicked yet, this is your window. The untold story of the Witches of Oz is magical, moving, and surprisingly family-friendly. 📍 Orpheum Theater, 409 S. 16th Street, Omaha

**Disney's Mary Poppins at Hawks Mainstage Theatre** (through June 28) "Supercalifragilistic" fun for every age. A beautiful production of the practically perfect nanny's adventures — dazzling choreography and songs kids will be singing all week. 📍 Hawks Mainstage Theatre, 6915 Cass St, Omaha

---

## 🌿 Outdoor Adventures & Free Summer Fun

**Papillion Days 2026** (June 17–21) ⭐ *Editor's Pick* One of the Omaha metro's most beloved traditions! Dating back to 1947, Papillion Days is a full week of community celebration — parade, fireworks, carnival rides, local market, and live entertainment. It's completely FREE and perfect for kids of all ages. Don't miss the fireworks Friday night! 📍 City Park, 84th & Lincoln, Papillion, NE

**Heartwood Kids Club** (Every Saturday through September) Free Saturday morning fun designed specifically for kids ages 4–8. A different craft, demo, or creative project every week — a wonderful way to start the weekend as a family. 📍 Heartwood Park, 301 S 145th St, Omaha | FREE

**Dinosaurs Around the World: Passport to Pangea** (through September 7) Life-sized animatronic dinosaurs that roar and move throughout the beautiful Lauritzen Gardens. Your kids will absolutely lose their minds over this one — and it runs all the way through Labor Day weekend! 📍 Lauritzen Gardens, 100 Bancroft St, Omaha

**Free Stinson Park Concert Series** (Every Saturday evening through summer) Pack lawn chairs, a blanket, and your cooler — Stinson Park hosts FREE live music every Saturday night all summer long. One of Omaha's great family traditions. 📍 Stinson Park, 2285 S. 67th St, Omaha | FREE

---

## 🙏 Faith-Based Family Events

**Rainforest Falls VBS — King of Kings Church** (June 22–26) A full week of faith, fun, and friendship! Kids discover who God is through Bible stories, games, crafts, and worship. One of the largest and most beloved VBS programs in the Omaha area. FREE for kids ages 5–12. 📍 King of Kings Church, Northwest Omaha

**Rainforest Falls VBS — Candlewood Church** (June 28 – July 2, evenings) Can't make King of Kings? Candlewood Church runs an evening VBS the following week — perfect for families with daytime commitments. 6–8pm each night. FREE. 📍 Candlewood Church, Omaha

**LOH Church VBS: Lean on Jesus** (June 27) An Italian-themed VBS day for kids ages 4 through 5th grade. A full day of faith and fellowship from 9am–2:30pm. FREE. 📍 LOH Church, Omaha

---

## 🍎 Fall Family Favorites (Plan Ahead!)

**Vala's Pumpkin Patch — Cider & Sunflower Festival** (September 2026) Omaha families know: fall means Vala's. Pick sunflowers, drink fresh cider, and enjoy one of the most magical family experiences in Nebraska. Dates will be announced soon — bookmark this one! 📍 Vala's Pumpkin Patch, Gretna, NE

**AppleJack Festival at Arbor Day Farm** (Sept 18–20 & Sept 26–27) Just a short drive from Omaha, the AppleJack Festival at Arbor Day Farm is Nebraska's premier apple harvest celebration. Apple picking, cider, hayrides, and gorgeous fall scenery. Perfect for families. 📍 Arbor Day Farm, Nebraska City, NE
