## Tips for the Perfect Omaha Family Movie Night

### Before You Go

1. **Check the weather** — Omaha summers are warm but occasional rain happens. Have a backup plan. 2. **Bring a blanket or chairs** — Grass is softer than concrete, but a cushion is always appreciated. 3. **Pack snacks smartly** — Many parks allow outside food and drinks. Skip the concession prices and bring your own popcorn! 4. **Arrive early** — Popular spots fill up 30–45 minutes before showtime. Come at 6 PM or earlier to secure a great viewing spot.

### During the Movie

- Keep kids entertained during pre-movie activities - Use the restroom before the feature starts - Keep a watchful eye on young children in crowded parks - Enjoy the *experience*—this is pure Omaha summer fun

### After the Movie

- Help keep the park clean—Omaha parks are beautiful because families respect them - Let kids run and play a bit before heading home (they'll sleep better!) - Say hi to neighbors—that's what community is all about
