The beloved Nelson Produce Farm in Valley transitions from its Sunflower Festival into full Fall Festival mode. Expect u-pick pumpkins, hayrack rides, a giant hay pyramid, over 50 pumpkin varieties, an apple cider slushie bar, caramel apples, donuts, and homemade syrups from the Coffee Barn. A perfect family farm day just 20 minutes from Omaha.
