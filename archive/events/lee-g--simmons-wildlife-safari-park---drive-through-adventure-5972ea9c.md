Just 30 minutes from Omaha near Ashland, the Lee G. Simmons Wildlife Safari Park is a 440-acre drive-through and walk-through wildlife park where families can see elk, bison, deer, wolves, bears, eagles, and more roaming spacious natural habitats. New for 2026, the park features 'Dinosaurs Unearthed: A Trek Through Time,' a walk-through exhibit with 22 life-sized animatronic dinosaurs. A fun, easy, all-ages outdoor adventure. Faith-based: no.
