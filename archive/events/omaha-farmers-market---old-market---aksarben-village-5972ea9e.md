A beloved Omaha tradition from May through October: the Omaha Farmers Market brings local growers, bakers, and makers together for a morning of fresh produce, flowers, baked goods, and family fun. Stroll the Old Market on Saturdays (8 AM–1 PM) or Aksarben Village on Sundays (9 AM–1 PM), grab breakfast, and support local. Free to walk around and browse. Ages: all ages. Faith-based: no. Cost: free admission (bring cash/cards for vendors). This listing's date reflects the end of the market season so it stays visible.
