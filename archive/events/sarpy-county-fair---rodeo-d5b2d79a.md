The 89th Annual Sarpy County Fair runs July 29 – August 2, 2026 in Springfield, NE. Five days of 4-H/FFA shows and exhibits, a carnival with unlimited-ride wristbands, truck & tractor pulls, rodeo, live music, and a Sunday demolition derby. Ages: all ages. Faith-based: no. Cost: grounds admission free; carnival wristbands ~$30; individual grandstand events $10–$25. Full run: July 29 – August 2, 2026.
