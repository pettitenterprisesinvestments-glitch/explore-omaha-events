Tunes on Town Square is Ralston's free community concert series, held the third Thursday of each month from May through September, 6 to 8 PM, at the new gazebo in downtown Ralston Town Square. Bring a chair and some snacks and enjoy a relaxed evening of live local music with friends and neighbors. Free admission, all ages welcome. Please note: per city ordinance, no alcoholic beverages may be consumed in public. July's featured musical guest is The Day Traders. 2026 lineup: May 21 – High Heel, June 18 – Back Alley, July 16 – The Day Traders, August 20 – One 2 Go, September 17 – Taxi Driver.
