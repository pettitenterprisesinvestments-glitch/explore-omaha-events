Voted Nebraska's Best Pumpkin Patch, Skinny Bones opens for its 18th season in Blair, just 30 minutes from Omaha. Attractions include a massive corn maze, hayrides, pumpkin picking, gem mining, a bounce pillow, kettle corn, caramel apples, and dozens of photo-worthy spots. Great for kids of all ages — runs through November 1.
