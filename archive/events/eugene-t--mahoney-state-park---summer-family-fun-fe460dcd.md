About 35 minutes from Omaha near Ashland, Eugene T. Mahoney State Park is a family-getaway favorite. Summer days are packed with the Family Aquatic Center and water slides, an indoor playground, paddle boats, mini-golf, hiking and biking trails, a nature conservatory, and the park's landmark observation tower with sweeping views. Horseback trail rides run Memorial Day weekend through Labor Day for ages 6 and up. A wholesome outdoor day for the whole family. Faith-based: no. Cost: a Nebraska park entry permit is required; some activities have additional fees.
