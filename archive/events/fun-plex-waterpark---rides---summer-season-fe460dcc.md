Nebraska's largest waterpark and ride park is open all summer long right here in Omaha. Cool off on towering waterslides, float the lazy river, splash in the wave pool, and let the little ones enjoy the kiddie play structures — then hit the dry rides for go-karts, bumper boats, bumper cars, and classic carnival fun. A go-to family destination on a hot Omaha day. Ages: all ages (great for kids). Faith-based: no. Cost: day passes / tickets — see the official site.
